About this app

Campus Sync is the all-in-one academic companion built specifically for engineering students. Stop juggling between spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, and paper registers — manage your entire semester from one beautifully designed app.

Whether you're tracking attendance before it drops below the 75% threshold, syncing your timetable across devices, or keeping up with exam schedules and assignment deadlines, Campus Sync has you covered — automatically.

Key Features:

  • Smart Attendance Tracker — real-time subject-wise attendance with alerts when you're at risk of dropping below the required percentage
  • Timetable Sync — import or manually create your class schedule and get timely class-start reminders
  • Assignment & Deadline Manager — never miss a submission with smart push notifications up to 24 hours before deadlines
  • CGPA Calculator — simulate grade scenarios and plan your semester target with an intuitive grade predictor
  • Notice Board — receive official college notices and department announcements directly in the app
  • Exam Scheduler — auto-populated exam timetable with countdown timers and revision reminders
  • Offline Mode — core features work without internet, perfect for slow campus Wi-Fi conditions
  • Minimal & Fast — lightweight app (~12 MB) with no unnecessary bloat, optimized for mid-range Android phones

Who is this for?

Campus Sync was originally built for B.Tech students at IIT Patna and has since grown to serve students across multiple universities. If your college follows a semester-based credit system, Campus Sync adapts perfectly to your workflow.
